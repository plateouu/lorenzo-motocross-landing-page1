ELEVATOR PITCH (Stage 4) - TAYLOR DAAN
----------------------------------------

"Hi everyone, my research focuses on the hidden gatekeepers of our digital lives: social media algorithms.

Right now, small creators are under intense psychological pressure. Instead of finding a space for original expression, they are being forced to abandon their authentic ideas just to chase virality. This isn't just about 'getting views'—it’s a fundamental shift in how creators view their own creative identities.

This led me to my core research question: How does algorithmic gatekeeping on platforms like TikTok and Instagram specifically impact the psychological well-being of small content creators?

To answer this, I’m launching a study using anonymous surveys to gather broad data. But more importantly, I’ll be conducting qualitative interviews to dive deep into the personal 'identity' and mental health of these creators. By using thematic coding, I can map their specific emotional experiences directly to algorithmic changes.

This study matters because it gets to the root of why our online communities are currently in a state of crisis—from engagement-farming and echo chambers to the overall mental health of digital spaces.

The biggest challenge? The algorithm itself hides the very people I need to interview: the small creators. So, my immediate next steps are to secure approval and start recruiting participants directly from independent creator forums. Thank you."
