ELEVATOR PITCH TALKING POINTS
AP Research (Stage 4) - Taylor Daan

1. THE PROBLEM (HOOK)
- Algorithms are the hidden gatekeepers of social media.
- For small creators, this creates massive psychological pressure.
- They aren't just making content; they're forced to abandon their original ideas to chase virality.

2. THE "WHY"
- Instead of encouraging original work, platforms force creators to change who they are creatively just to "fit the algorithm."
- This causes real-world stress and an identity crisis for new users.

3. THE RESEARCH QUESTION
- My goal is to answer: "How does algorithmic gatekeeping on TikTok and Instagram specifically impact the psychological well-being of these small creators?"

4. MY PLAN (METHOD)
- I’m launching into data collection using anonymous surveys for broad data.
- Then, I'll do qualitative interviews to really get into the "identity" and personal stories of these creators.
- I'll map these psychological experiences directly to algorithmic events using thematic coding.

5. THE BIG PICTURE (SIGNIFICANCE)
- This study matters because it gets to the root of why our online communities are in crisis.
- It explains engagement-farming, echo chambers, and the mental health issues we see every day online.

6. CHALLENGES & NEXT STEPS
- The biggest hurdle? The algorithm hides the very people I need to interview (small creators).
- My next step is getting approval and digging into creator forums to find my participants.
